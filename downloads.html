Downloads

The most convenient way to get matplotlib is to use a package management tool as described in the installation instructions.

If, however, you really need to access the downloads directly, they are available on PyPI.

Older releases, prior to version 1.2.0, are available on SourceForge.

As of matplotlib 1.5, we are no longer making downloads available on SourceForge.
